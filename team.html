Meet The Team

Martin

Martin Madar is the RV repair man; he heads the team and has an extensive knowledge of automobile repair, trailer function, and mechanics. Specializing in RV and camper van vehicles, Martin most enjoys the specific challenges that come with vehicle home repairs and maintenance. I mean, who doesn't enjoy building something that will experience and must withstand constant vibration from roads? (Everyone except Martin, maybe?) He's also quite helpful with carpentry and tends to be an all-around handyman.

Martin has been steadily building this company and brand over the years, prioritizing 'honest work' throughout his services. He has hand-picked the individuals that work with him, desiring both knowledgeable tradesmen and transparent communicators. These qualities indeed form the base ideology of the company, which is most simply stated as a dedication to honest, knowledgeable service.

Through the years he's gained a wide variety of skills and specialties, which you can check out a comprehensive list of here. While he has so much experience in the RV domain, he still truly enjoys the challenges that come with new, unexpected jobs. He's been learning all these skills since his youth, which you can read more about here, in an article written by Voyage LA.




Troy

As one of Martin's personal friends, Troy has had the great joy of watching Martin build his business from the ground up. But Troy also happens to be a knowledgeable businessman and consultant, which is exactly why he aids Martin in handling the RV repair guy company. by stepping in to offer business insight, handle scheduling, and perform specific administrative duties.
