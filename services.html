Solar Panels



If you're interested in boondocking or off-grid living, then a personal power system is going to be a priority. We specialize in installation, maintenance, repair and sale of independent renewable energy sources.

If you're looking to purchase one of our comprehensive solar kits, check 'em out here. We love those kits, and we've narrowed the options down to the five most useful sizes in terms of solar panel wattage and battery bank storage. We've also focused on compiling these kits to use only the highest quality parts from American-made manufacturers.

Otherwise we can help you find, choose, and/or install any customized solar system to meet your specific needs. Get in touch with us to discuss your own system and get the ball rollin'.

Vanlife Schoolings


There is so much involved with vanlife, and the community is one that welcomes you with open arms! As a member of that community I am here to help you out in any way, whether it's figuring out how you want to attack this change of pace, figuring out how your new van or vehicle is going to meet all your needs while out on the road, or even just finding other vanlifers to talk with and share ideas/parking spots with.

There are so many wonderful resources a quick google search could give you, and any beach front public parking will also probably land you in the middle of a vanlife 'neighborhood' where you can talk with others to learn more. Let me know if you need specific guidance or help from my part.




RV Rentals & Resale

You might be looking to rent, rent out, or sell your vehicle.

If you're looking to rent or rent-out a vehicle, then I can connect you with potential renters or rentees in the area. I also have a variety of rental options depending on your specific needs. I can help you decide which rental suits your trip, because you're going to want a different vehicle if you're headed to the middle of nowhere versus a trip from city to city.

For the more free-spirited among you, we also offer Burning Man specific rental options.

If you're interested in selling your vehicle, I'm able to connect you with different potential buyers, perform any necessary vehicle inspections for you, and guide you through the general process involved with selling a camper van or recreational vehicle.

Get in touch to discuss rental options further.
